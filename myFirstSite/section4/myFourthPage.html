The After Math

After winning the '98 World Series, The Yankees proceeded to complete what is known today as the '3Peat'. After these straight wins they knew where the luck was from. It was the Yankee Baby! It was the miracle of this powerful child that was born the night of the 1st game of the World Series. Instead of taking advantage of this baby they decided to mentor the child and help him use him powers for good in baseball. They officially pronounced that this miracle Yankee child "Had The Power!"

As the Yankee Baby grew older, he too found the love of the sport, Baseball. He knew with his living miracle of the Yankee team and their support he could accomplish and win to! The pronouced him the most powerful Yankee of them all. He had the power to produce wins and be the best baseball player ever, due to his birth miracle. The Yankee organization assigned him an outstanding logo that signified the City, The Team and his Powers!
